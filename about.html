Our Story

Built on Honesty & Hard Work.

Deluxe Scrap Buyers was founded with one simple belief: every person who sells scrap deserves a fair deal. No tricks, no last-minute rate changes, no bargaining games.

Based in Musheerabad, Hyderabad, we started as a small operation serving our local neighbourhood. Today, we serve customers across Hyderabad — from Secunderabad to Bholakpur, from Kavadiguda to Ameerpet — all with the same commitment to transparency and speed.

We invested in certified digital weighing scales early on, because we believe you should see every gram being counted. That one decision changed everything. Customers started trusting us. Reviews started pouring in. Today we have a 5.0 rating on Google with 123 verified reviews.

We accept iron, steel, copper, aluminium, brass, e-waste, paper, cardboard, and plastic. We come to you, weigh everything in front of you, and pay instantly — cash or UPI. That's the Deluxe promise.
